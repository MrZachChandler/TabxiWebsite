Ride Sharing Made Simple

Create Tabs

Creating a tab is an easy way to track miles, generate expense reports, or add users to your tab so they can ride for free.

Earn Points

Earning points everytime you ride helps support the drivers and the riders. It creates opportunities to pay for you tab with points or discounts for future rides.

Send Gifts.

Upload money to your virtual TabXi Bank. You can send money or points through the app.

More features

Your tabs are charged on a weekly basis, so you have plenty of time to send that expense report over to get it covered. You can create as many tabs as you need to cover all of your business or personal rides.

Have kids? Send money inside the app to exclusive cover their ride sharing needs; no more drunk driving, no more parking tickets, no more wasted money.
